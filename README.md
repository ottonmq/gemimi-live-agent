## 🛰️ OVERVIEW
**Otto-task** is more than a marketplace; it is an autonomous trade infrastructure managed by **Shadow Agent**, a multimodal AI core integrated into a Django backend. In the Otto-task bunker, inventory breathes, prices adapt, and sales are negotiated autonomously via high-speed neural processing.

---

## 💎 THE 3 MASTER IDEAS (HACKATHON PROTOCOL)

### 1. 🤝 AGENTIC NEGOTIATOR (Autonomous Sales Rep)
Shadow acts as a 24/7 sales representative. It’s not a static chatbot; it’s a tactical negotiator.
* **The Power:** If a user offers a lower price for an asset (e.g., a Toyota Hilux), Shadow analyzes the profit margins defined by the Architect and autonomously decides whether to accept, reject, or launch a counter-offer based on real-time market trends.
* **Innovation:** AI that protects ROI 24/7 without human intervention.

### 2. 📡 PREDICTIVE INVENTORY RADAR (Scarcity Intelligence)
Shadow scans the data ecosystem and the injected inventory to detect gaps in the local market.
* **The Power:** "Architect Dmfhdilyd, Redmi M15 stock is zero in Santa Ana today." The agent suggests proactive price adjustments (+5% margin) or alerts for restock before the competition reacts.
* **Innovation:** Proactive market intelligence delivered directly from the terminal.


### 3. 🎬 MULTIMODAL SUPPORT: LIVE VIDEO GENERATION
Leveraging the multimodal power of **Gemini 2.0 Flash**, Shadow transforms static photos into dynamic marketing content.
* **The Power:** Upon uploading a product, Shadow detects technical specs and automatically generates a video clip with voiceover and highlighted text.
* **Innovation:** Total visual marketing automation from a simple Ubuntu terminal.

## 🚀 REPRODUCIBILITY PROTOCOL (JUDGES ONLY)
To sync with the Shadow Core:
1. **Clone & Install:**
   `pip install -r requirements.txt`
2. **Environment Setup:**
   Create a `.env` file with your `GOOGLE_API_KEY`.
3. **Initialize Bunker:**
   `python manage.py migrate`
   `python manage.py runserver`
4. **Live Test:**
   Ask Shadow: *"Identify item 51 brand and model."*
   **Expected Result:** Shadow will perform grounding and return "XIOMI A15".
